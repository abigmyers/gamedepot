This is a nonogram, the numbers next to each row or column determine how many spaces of said row or column are marked. If there is more than one number marking a row or column, then that means that there is an open space in between those spaces. So if you see [2, 2], then you know the first 2 boxes and the last two boxes are filled in, and the middle is open.
